Design, for me, begins with atmosphere — the deliberate construction of emotion through material, light, and proportion.

I approach each project as an emotional narrative, where concept shapes experience and space becomes something felt rather than simply seen.

Like Philip Johnson, I am not confined to a single aesthetic language. I am interested in the dialogue between styles — not as inconsistency, but as exploration driven by intention.
